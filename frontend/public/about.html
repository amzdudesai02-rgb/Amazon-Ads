About this project

Amazon Ads AI Agent is an assistant that helps advertisers explore and manage their Amazon Ads campaigns using natural language. It connects to Amazon Ads through Login with Amazon (LWA) and the Amazon Ads API, and uses a large language model to turn your prompts into useful insights and actions.

Typical use cases include generating campaign reports, surfacing optimization ideas, and helping you scale campaigns to new markets.

How it works

  • You connect your Amazon Ads account via Login with Amazon.
  • The backend securely stores tokens and retrieves campaign data from the Amazon Ads API.
  • The AI model analyzes your data and questions to generate tailored responses.
