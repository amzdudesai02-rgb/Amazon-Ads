Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how the Amazon Ads AI Agent (“we”, “our”, or “the Service”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use our application.

1. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect:

  • Amazon Ads data: campaign, ad group, keyword, and performance data retrieved via the Amazon Ads API after you grant access.
  • Login with Amazon tokens: access and refresh tokens required to authorize requests to the Amazon Ads API.
  • Usage data: chat messages you send to the agent, and the responses generated, which we may store to improve the Service.

2. How we use information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate with Amazon Ads and maintain your connection.
  • Retrieve and analyze your Amazon Ads data in order to answer your questions and perform actions you request.
  • Improve the quality, reliability, and security of the Service (for example, by monitoring errors and performance).

3. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We may share data only with the following parties:

  • Service providers that host infrastructure, databases, and AI models (for example, cloud hosting or language model APIs), strictly for the purpose of providing the Service.
  • Amazon, to the extent required to call the Amazon Ads API and comply with Amazon’s policies.
  • When required by law, to comply with a valid legal request or to protect our rights.

4. Data retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the Service and meet legal or contractual obligations. You may contact us to request deletion of certain stored data, subject to technical and legal limitations.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, such as encrypted connections (HTTPS) and restricted access to sensitive credentials. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Your choices

  • You can revoke our access to your Amazon Ads account at any time via your Amazon account settings.
  • You may stop using the Service at any time. You can also contact us to request deletion of stored data where feasible.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Material changes may also be communicated through the app or by other means.
