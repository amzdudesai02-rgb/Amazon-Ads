Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how AMZDUDES ("we", "our", or "the Service") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our Amazon Ads AI Agent, MCP integrations, and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Amazon Ads data: campaign, ad group, keyword, targeting, performance, billing, and related account data retrieved through the Amazon Ads API after you grant access.
  • Authentication data: Login with Amazon (LwA) credentials metadata, access tokens, refresh tokens, and related authorization information required to connect to the Amazon Ads API.
  • Usage data: prompts, chat messages, requests, generated responses, tool interactions, and general usage activity within the Service.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system, error logs, diagnostics, and performance monitoring data.
  • Contact information: information you provide when contacting us for support or making a data request.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Authenticate your connection to Amazon Ads and maintain your account integration.
  • Retrieve, analyze, and present Amazon Ads data in response to your requests.
  • Enable AI-powered insights, reporting, recommendations, and workflow automation.
  • Operate, maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the Service.
  • Monitor performance, detect abuse, prevent fraud, and improve security.
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, contractual, and platform requirements.

3. Legal Basis and Authorization

Where applicable, we process your information because it is necessary to provide the Service, fulfill our contractual obligations, pursue legitimate business interests such as security and service improvement, comply with legal obligations, or based on your consent and authorization.

By connecting your Amazon Ads account, you authorize us to access and process the data made available through the Amazon Ads API in accordance with your permissions and Amazon's platform requirements.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: with trusted vendors who help us host, secure, monitor, and operate the Service, including infrastructure, analytics, and AI processing providers acting on our behalf.
  • Amazon: where necessary to provide Amazon Ads API functionality and comply with Amazon requirements.
  • Legal compliance: if required by law, regulation, court order, or valid governmental request.
  • Business protection: to enforce our terms, protect rights, investigate fraud, or address security issues.
  • Business transfers: in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate safeguards.

5. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the Service, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

  • Amazon Ads data may be retained for reporting, analytics, and service functionality.
  • Authentication tokens may be retained for as long as needed to maintain your active connection.
  • Usage logs, prompts, and system diagnostics may be retained for operational, quality, and security purposes.

When information is no longer required, we delete, anonymize, or securely dispose of it where feasible.

6. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, including HTTPS, restricted access controls, credential handling protections, and monitoring measures. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. International Data Processing

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your own, including where our service providers operate. Where applicable, we take reasonable steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for cross-border data transfers.

8. Your Choices and Rights

You may have the following rights, subject to applicable law:

  • Request access to information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request deletion of your information, where applicable.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing in some circumstances.
  • Withdraw authorization by revoking our access to your Amazon Ads account.

You can revoke our access to your Amazon Ads account at any time through your Amazon account settings, where available. You may also contact us directly to submit privacy-related requests.

9. Third-Party Services

The Service may rely on third-party platforms and providers, including Amazon Ads, Login with Amazon, hosting providers, analytics tools, and AI service providers. Your use of those services may also be subject to their own terms and privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under the age of 13, or a higher minimum age where required by applicable law. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. Material changes may be communicated through the Service or by other reasonable means.

12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at: support@amzdudes.io
